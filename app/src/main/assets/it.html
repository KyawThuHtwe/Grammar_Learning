Emphatic Writing


  • ဝါကျထဲရှိ မိမိအထူးပြုလိုသောစကားလုံး/ စကားစုများကို ရှေ့သို့ထုတ်ပြီး ရေးသည့်ပုံစံကို emphatic writing ဟု ခေါ်သည်
  • ဝါကျကို It is/ was ဖြင့် အစပြုသည်
  • It is/ was နောက်တွင် မိမိအထူးပြုလိုသည့် စကားစုကိုရေးပါ... မိမိအထူးပြုလိုသည့် စကားစုဆိုသည်မှာ Subject, Object, adverb phrase of time, adverb phrase of place များ ဖြစ်ကြသည်
  • မိမိအထူးပြုလိုသည့် စကားစုသည် လူဖြစ်လျင် who နှင့်ဆက်ပြီး လူမဟုတ်လျင် that နှင့်ဆက်ပါ
  • ကျန်သည့်အပိုင်းဆက်ကူးပါ

Examples:
1. Nilar bought a dictionary from the new book store last week.
It was Nilar who bought a dictionary from the new book store last week.
It was a dictionary that Nilar bought from the new book store last week.
It was from the new book store that Nilar bought a dictionary last week.
It was last week that Nilar bought a dictionary from the new book store.
2. Mangoes are plentiful during the rainy season.
It is mangoes that are plentiful during the rainy season.
It is during the rainy season that mangoes are plentiful.
Exercises
1. Min Min discovered that the room had been broken into.

▶ It was Min Min .....

It was Min Min who discovered that the room had been broken into.

2. The doctor at the Myo clinic helped her brother to walk again.

▶ It was .....

It was the doctor at the Myo clinic who helped her brother to walk again.

3. The patient suffers when the doctor makes a mistake.

▶ It is the patient .....

It is the patient who suffers when the doctor makes a mistake.

4. I don' t like her attitude towards work.

▶ It is her attitude towards work .....

It is her attitude towards work that I don't like.

5. This company operates efficiently and profitably this year.

▶ It is this year .....

It is this year that this company operates efficiently and profitable this year.

6. The heart supplies the whole body with its life-blood.

▶ It is the heart .....

It is the heart that supplies the whole body with its life-blood.

7. MRTV 4 broadcasts the latest and breaking news to the public.

▶ It is .....

It is MRTV 4 that broadcasts the latest and breaking news to the public.

8. Learning English is very hard.

▶ It is learning English .....

It is learning English that is very hard.

9. Ko Lay suggested that we should go to Bagan first.

▶ It was .....

It was Ko Lay who suggested that we should go to Bagan first.

10. The study of human white blood cells led to discoveries of treatments in fighting AIDS.

▶ It was .....

It was the study of human white blood cells that led to treatments in fighting AIDS.
