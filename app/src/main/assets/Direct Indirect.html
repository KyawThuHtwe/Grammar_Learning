Direct speech နှင့် Indirect speech

လူတစ်ဦးတစ်ယောက် ပြောသည့်စကားကို သူပြောသည့်အတိုင်း ပြန်ပြောပြသည့်ပုံစံကို direct speech ဟုခေါ်သည်
◾He said, " I'm hungry. "

လူတစ်ဦးတစ်ယောက် ပြောသည့်စကားကို တစ်ဆင့် ပြန်ပြောပြသည့်ပုံစံကို indirect speech ဟုခေါ်သည်
◾He said that he was hungry.

direct speech မှ indirect speech သို့ပြောင်းရာတွင် အဓိကအားဖြင့် pronoun,verb နှင့် adverb phrases တို့ကိုပြောင်းပေးရသည်


  • direct speech မှ indirect speech သို့ပြောင်းရာတွင် အပြောခံရသူမပါလျင် said ကို that နှင့်ဆက်ပါ
  • အပြောခံရသူပါလျင် said to ကို told ပြောင်းပြီး told + အပြောခံရသူ + that ပုံစံရေးပါ
  • ဝါကျရှိ comma( , ) နှင့် inverted comma( " " ) များ ဖြုတ်ပါ
  • I, me , my , mine , myself တို့ပါလျင် ပြောသူနှင့်ညှိပါ
    you , you , your , yours , yourself ပါလျင် အပြောခံရသူနှင့်ညှိပါ

  • အချို့သော အချိန်နှင့် နေရာပြကြိယာဝိသေသနများကို အောက်ပါအတိုင်း ပြောင်းပေးရသည်

Examples:
1. Ma Ma said, "I want to be a doctor when I grow up."
Ma Ma said that she wanted to be a doctor when she grew up.
2. Ko Ko said, "I am doing my homework now."
Ko Ko said that he was doing his homework then.
3. Maung Maung said to Nilar, "I will return your book tomorrow."
Maung Muang told Nilar that he would return her book the next day.
4. Ma Ma said to Tun Tun, "You can borrow my laptop if you promise to use it carefully."
Ma Ma told Tun Tun that he could borrow her laptop if he promised to use it carefully.
5. The teacher said, "Thiha, I think you are just too lazy to pass the exam."
The teacher told Thiha that she thought he was just too lazy to pass the exam.
6. Marlar said to her brother, "The program you want to watch finished fifteen minutes ago."
Marlar told her brother that the program he wanted to watch had finished fifteen minutes before.
7. Father said to me, "I am not going to buy you anything if you don't get good grades in the test this month."
Father told me that he was not going to buy me anything if I did not get good grades in the test that month.
8. Nilar said to me, "You have helped me in every way you can."
Nilar told me that I had helped her in every way I could.
9. Naing Lin said to Marlar, "We are planning to go on a trip to a place you are very much interested in."
Naing Lin told Marlar that they were planning to go on a trip to a place she was very much interested in.
10. Phyu Phyu said to her mother, "Our teacher told us a very inspiring story today."
Phyu Phyu told her mother that their teacher had told them a very inspiring story that day.
11. The teacher said to the students, "I am sure you will have more confidence in yourselves after taking this test."
The teacher told the students that she was sure they would have more confidence in themselves after taking that test.
Command and request(အမိန့်ပေးတောင်းပန်ဝါကျများ)

  • အမိန့်ပေး တောင်းပန်ဝါကျများကို V1, Please + V1, Don' t + V1 တို့ဖြင့်အစပြုသည်
  • ဝါကျကို V1 ဖြင့်အစပြုထားလျင် to နှင့်ဆက်ပါ
  • ဝါကျကို Don' t + V1 ဖြင့်အစပြုထားလျင် not to နှင့် ဆက်ပါ
  • please ပါလျင်ဖြုတ်ပါ
  • ကျန်ပြောင်းလဲပုံမှာအတူတူဖြစ်သည်
Examples:
1. Thida said to Tun Tun, "Please help me with my homework."
Thida told Tun Tun to help her with her homework.
2. Mother said to me, "Read your answers carefully before you give them to the teacher."
Mother told me to read my answers carefully before I gave them to the teacher.
3. The girl said to the boy, "Don't come near me or I will shout out loud."
The girl told the boy not to come near her or she would shout out loud.
4. Ko Ko said to Thuzar, "Please don't leave me alone."
Ko Ko told Thuzar not to leave him alone.
Questions(အမေးဝါကျများ)

  • အပြောခံရသူမပါလျင် said ကို wanted to know ပြောင်းပါ
  • အပြောခံရသူပါလျင် said to ကို asked ပြောင်းပါ
  • မေးခွန်းကိုအကူကြိယာဖြင့်အစပြုထားလျင် if/ whether နှင့်ဆက်ပါ
  • မေးခွန်းကို Wh- စကားလုံးဖြင့် အစပြုထားလျင် ၎င်း Wh- စကားလုံးကို ပြန်သုံးပေးရသည်
  • အမေးဝါကျရှိ helping verb + subject ပုံစံကို subject + helping verb ပုံစံပြန်ပြောင်းပေးရသည်
  • question mark ( ? ) ပြန်ထည့်စရာမလိုပါ
  • ကျန်ပြောင်းလဲပုံမှာအတူတူဖြစ်သည်
Examples:
1. Hnin Hnin said, "Will the rain stop today?"
Hnin Hnin wanted to know if the rain would stop that day
2. Nyi Nyi said, "Can I take the medicine if I haven't eaten anything?"
Nyi Nyi wanted to know whether he could take the medicine if he had not eaten anything.
3. Tun Tun said, "Why did I do such a stupid thing?"
Tun Tun wanted to know why he had done such a stupid thing.
4. Mother said to me, "Are you going out tonight?"
Mother asked me if I was going out that night.
5. Thura said to Ni Ni, "Why do you think you are better than me?"
Thura asked Ni Ni why she thought she was better than him.
6. Sandi said to Ko Myo, "What have you planned for our trip?"
Sandi asked Ko Myo what he had planned for their trip.
7. The old man said to the boy, "Can you tell me where the monastery is?"
The old man asked the boy if he could tell him where the monastery was.
Exercises
1. Moe Moe said, "I will see you tomorrow, Aye Aye."

▶ Moe Moe told .....

Moe Moe told Aye Aye that she would see her the next day.

2. Father said to us, "I want all of you to be punctual."

▶ Father told .....

Father told us that he wanted all of us to be punctual.

3. Mother said, "Htwe, Htwe, I don't like you wearing your sister's clothes."

▶ Mother told .....

Mother told Htwe Htwe that she did not like her wearing her sister's clothes.

4. Thandar said to Nandar, "Don't be late for the meeting tomorrow."

▶ Thandar told .....

Thandar told Nandar not to be late for the meeting the next day.

5. The teacher said, "Thura, why didn't you attend my class yesterday?"

▶ The teacher asked .....

The teacher asked Thura why he had not attended her class the day before.

6. Mu Mu said to me, "How long have you been in your present job?"

▶ Mu Mu asked me .....

Mu Mu asked me how long I had been in my present job.

7. The teacher said to Hla Hla, "You can sit here."

▶ The teacher told .....

The teacher told Hla Hla that she could sit there.

8. The teacher said, "Where have you been all day, Mg Mg?"

▶ The teacher asked .....

The teacher asked Mg Mg where I had been all day.

9. Mother said, "Aung Aung, I don't know what you are talking about."

▶ Mother told .....

Mother told Aung Aung that she did not know what he was talking about.

10. Ko Ko said to Thuzar, "I will send you a postcard as soon as I reach China."

▶ Ko Ko told .....

Ko Ko told Thuzar that he would send her a postcard as soon as he reached China.

11. Aye Aye said to Hla Hla, "Do you know when the concert will start?"

▶ Aye Aye wanted to know .....

Aye Aye wanted to know from Hla Hla if she knew when the concert would start.

12. The little boy said to his teacher, "Can I leave now?"

▶ The little boy asked .....

The little boy asked his teacher if he could leave then.

13. Our teacher said to us, "Study hard if you want to get high marks in the examination."

▶ Our teacher advised us .....

Our teacher advised us to study hard if we wanted to get high marks in the examination.

14. I said to my brother, "What happened to your bicycle?"

▶ I asked my brother .....

I asked my brother what had happened to his bicycle.

15. The teacher said to Zaw Zaw, "If you don't change your study habits, you won't pass your examination."

▶ The teacher told .....

The teacher told Zaw Zaw that if he did not change his study habits, he would not pass his examination.
