too ... to ⇄ not ... enough to


  • too ... to/ not ... enough to သည် တစ်စုံတစ်ယောက်က တစ်စုံတစ်ရာပြုလုပ်ရန်အတွက် အရည်အသွေးမရှိကြောင်း ဖော်ပြသည့် ပုံစံဖြစ်သည်
  • model တစ်ယောက်ဖြစ်နိုင်လောက်အောင် အရပ်မမြင့်ဘူး , စာအုပ်က မဝယ်နိုင်လောက်အောင် ဈေးကြီးတယ် စသည့် ပုံစံများဖြစ်သည်
  • too ... to မှ not ... enough to သို့ ပြောင်းရာတွင် too + adj ကို not + adj(ဆန့်ကျင်ဘက်) + enough ပုံစံပြောင်းပါ... ကျန်သည့်အပိုင်း ပြောင်းစရာမလိုပါ
  • not ... enough to မှ too ... to သို့ပြောင်းရာတွင် not + adj + enough ပုံစံကို too + adj(ဆန့်ကျင်ဘက်) ပုံစံပြောင်းပြီး ကျန်သည့်အပိုင်း ဆက်ကူးပါ
Examples:
1. Ni Ni is too short to become a model.
Ni Ni is not tall enough to become a model.
2. This handset is too expensive for me to buy.
This handset is not cheap enough for me to buy.
3. Tun Tun was not well enough to go to school that day.
Tun Tun was too ill to go to school that day.
4. The story was not interesting enough for her to continue reading.
The story was too boring for her to continue reading.
Exercises
1. The problem is not easy enough for us to solve.

▶ The problem is too .....

The problem is too difficult for us to solve.

2. My sister is too weak to attend the class today.

▶ My sister is not .....

My sister is not strong enough to attend the class today.

3. The child is too young to look after himself.

▶ The child is not .....

The child is not old enough to look after himself.

4. The rope is not long enough to reach the ground.

▶ The rope is too .....

The rope is too short to reach the ground.

5. He is too poor to own a big house.

▶ He is not .....

He is not rich enough to own a big house.

6. Khine Maw is not tall enough to be in the basketball team.

▶ Khine Maw is too .....

Khine Maw is too short to be in the basketball team.

7. The road is not wide enough for these containers.

▶ The road is too .....

The road is too narrow for these containers.

8. The film was too boring for me to continue watching.

▶ The film was not .....

The film was not interesting enough for me to continue watching.

9. Sandar felt too ill to go on a trip to Bagan.

▶ Sandar did not feel .....

Sandar did not feel well enough to go on a trip to Bagan.

10. The box is too heavy for him to lift alone.

▶ The box is not .....

The box is not light enough for him to lift alone.
