Not only ... but also


  • Not only ... but also သည် ဝါကျနှစ်ကြောင်းရှိ အမျိုးအစားတူ စကားလုံး/ စကားစု နှစ်ခုကို ဆက်သွယ်ပေးသည်
  • Subject များကို Not only ... but also နှင့်ဆက်လျင် Verb အနည်းအများကို but also နောက်ရှိ Subject နှင့်ညှိပါ
  • Neither ... nor ဝါကျများကို Not only ... but also သို့ပြောင်းရာတွင် Verb ကို အငြင်းပြောင်းပေးရသည်
  • ဝါကျရှိ and, too, also, as well စသည့် စကားဆက်များ ဖြုတ်ပေးရသည်
Extra info on not only ... but also

Not only ... but also ကို Inversion ပုံစံနှင့်လည်း ရေးသားနိုင်သည်

◾Not only did he forget his wallet, but he left the keys inside.
◾Not only has she done the cooking, but she has washed the clothes.
◾Not only does it save time, but it also saves work.
( နဝမတန်း English ဖတ်စာအုပ် Unit 1: The Wheel ထဲမှဝါကျဖြစ်သည် )

Not only ... but also သည် စာဆန်ပြီး စကားပြောဆိုရာတွင် အသုံးနည်းသည်... Not only ... but also ကို ပုံစံကွဲအနေဖြင့် also ချန်ပြီးရေးသလို Not just ... but ပုံစံလည်း သုံးကြသည်

◾They were not only friendly but helpful.
◾It is not just Mandalay but Magway and Myingyan that are famous for their intense heat.


Not only ... but also ကို also ဖြုတ်ပြီးရေးထားသော သတင်းတစ်ပုဒ်၏ခေါင်းစဉ်...
BBC မှ ထုတ်လုပ်သော သဘာဝပတ်ဝန်းကျင်အကြောင်း ရိုက်ကူးထားသည့် Planet Earth II ထဲမှ နောက်ခံစကားပြောသူ သဘာဝပတ်ဝန်းကျင် ကျွမ်းကျင်သူတစ်ဦးဖြစ်သည့် Sir David Attenborough ပြောခဲ့သော စကားများထဲမှ not just ... but အသုံး

It's surely our responsibility to do everything within our power to create a planet that provides a home not just for us, but for all life on Earth.

Examples:
1. We are going to Pyin Oo Lwin this summer. We are going to Ngapali, too.
We are going not only to Pyin Oo Lwin but also to Ngapali this summer.
2. Nyi Nyi wants a laptop for his birthday. He also wants a bike for his birthday.
Nyi Nyi wants not only a laptop but also a bike for his birthday.
3. Ko Ko and his sister are good at English.
Not only Ko Ko but also his sister is good at English.
4. Neither Aung Aung nor Tun Tun went to school yesterday.
Not only Aung Aung but also Tun Tun did not go to school yesterday.
Exercises
1. Ko Ko has visited Japan as well as Hong Kong.

▶ Ko Ko has visited not only .....

Ko Ko has visited not only Japan but also Hong Kong.

2. Mother is very understanding. She is also very patient.

▶ Mother is not only .....

Mother is not only very understanding but also very patient.

3. Neither Toe Toe nor Soe Soe came to class today.

▶ Not only .....

Not only Toe Toe but also Soe Soe did not come to class today.

4. Helen was blind and deaf after a mysterious illness.

▶ Helen became not only .....

Helen became not only blind but also deaf after a mysterious illness.

5. I have read the novel "Gone with the Wind". I have seen the film, too.

▶ I have not only .....

I have not only read the novel "Gone with the Wind" but also seen the film.

6. Human beings can't live on the moon. Animals can't live on the moon.

▶ Not only .....

Not only human beings but also animals can't live on the moon.

7. She has done her cooking. She has washed the clothes.

▶ She has not only .....

She has not only done her cooking but also washed the clothes.

8. A bus driver is responsible for himself as well as for the safety of the passengers.

▶ A bus driver is .....

A bus driver is not only responsible for himself but also for the safety of the passengers.

9. Both Pinky and her sister are in our class.

▶ Not only .....

Not only Pinky but also her sister is in our class.

10. She likes nice clothes. She also likes lovely pieces of jewellery.

▶ She likes not only .....

She likes not only nice clothes but also lovely pieces of jewellery.
