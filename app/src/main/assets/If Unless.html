If ⇌ Unless


  • conditional သုံးမျိုးရှိသည့်အနက် type one ကို if မှ unless သို့ အပြန်အလှန် ပြောင်းခိုင်းသည့်ပုံစံသည် ကျောင်းသားများကို အမေးအများဆုံးဖြစ်သည်
    • ▶ Click here to learn about conditionals
  • unless သည် if ... not ဟုအဓိပ္ပါယ်ရသည်
  • unless သည် အငြင်းအဓိပ္ပါယ်ရှိသောကြောင့် if မှ unless သို့ အပြန်အလှန် ပြောင်းရာတွင် if/ unless clause နှင့် main clause ရှိ Verb များကို လိုအပ်သလို ပြောင်းပေးရသည်
  • if-clause တွင် want to ပါပြီး main clause တွင် must ပါလျင် must ကိုဖြုတ်ပြီး unless ဖြင့် စရေးပေးရသည်... want to ကို will not ပြောင်းပေးရသည်
  • unless မှ if သို့ပြောင်းလဲရာတွင် if-clause ရှိ Verb ကို အငြင်းပုံစံလုပ်ခြင်းသည် အကောင်းဆုံးပုံစံဖြစ်သည်
  • အမိန့်ပေး ခိုင်းစေဝါကျများ( Commands ) ကို unlessသို့ ပြောင်းလဲရာတွင် Subject ( You ) ထည့်ပေးရသည်... or ကိုဖြုတ်ပြီး comma ( , ) ခံပေးရသည်... ကျန်သည့်အပိုင်း ပြောင်းစရာမလိုပါ
Examples:
1. If you know how to use a computer, you should buy one.
Unless you know how to use a computer, you should not buy one.
2. If you stay away from classes, you will not pass the exam.
Unless you stay away from classes, you will pass the exam.
3. If you do not practise letters and essays, you will not get a distinction in English.
Unless you practise letters and essays, you will not get a distinction in English.
4. If Nyi Nyi does not find a job soon, his family will get into difficulties.
Unless Nyi Nyi finds a job soon, his family will get into difficulties.
5. If you want to get good marks, you must study hard.
Unless you study hard, you will not get good marks.
6. Thiha must stop smoking if he wants to be healthy.
Unless Thiha stops smoking, he will not be healthy.
7. Unless the teacher gives us lots of homework, we will watch television till midnight.
If the teacher does not give us lots of homework, we will watch television till midnight.
8. Unless you keep your promise, you will not be trusted.
If you do not keep your promise, you will not be trusted.
9. Do your homework now or I will beat you.
Unless you do your homework now, I will beat you.
10. Come early or we will not wait for you.
Unless you come early, we will not wait for you.
Exercises
1. Eat less or you will put on weight.

▶ Unless .....

Unless you eat less, you will put on weight.

2. I won't wake up if the alarm does not go off.

▶ Unless .....

Unless the alarm goes off, I won't wake up.

3. If you don't want to be late, you must hurry up.

▶ Unless .....

Unless you hurry up, you will be late.

4. If she doesn't know your address, she won't come.

▶ Unless .....

Unless she knows your address, she won't come.

5. Unless you know how to use a computer, you will not get a job easily.

▶ If .....

If you know how to use a computer, you will get a job easily.
(OR)
If you do not know how to use a computer, you will not get a job easily.

6. If you study hard, you will not fail the examination.

▶ Unless .....

Unless you study hard, you will fail the examination.

7. Unless we have passports, we can't go abroad.

▶ If .....

If we have passports, we can go abroad.
(OR)
If we don't have passports, we can't go abroad.

8. Unless you get up early, you will miss the train.

▶ If .....

If you get up early, you won't miss the train.
(OR)
If you don't get up early, you will miss the train.

9. If you do not attend the class regularly, you will be punished.

▶ Unless .....

Unless you attend the class regularly, you will be punished.

10. Stop arguing with your sister or you'll be beaten by your mother.

▶ Unless .....

Unless you stop arguing with your sister, you'll be beaten by your mother.
