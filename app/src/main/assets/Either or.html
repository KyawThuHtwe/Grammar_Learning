Either ... or


  • either ... or သည် ဝါကျနှစ်ကြောင်းရှိ အမျိုးအစားတူ စကားလုံး/ စကားစု နှစ်ခုကို ဆက်သွယ်ပေးသည်
  • either ... or သည် ရွေးခြယ်မှုနှစ်ခုကို ဖော်ပြသည်
  • subject များကို either ... or နှင့်ဆက်လျင် verb အနည်းအများကို or နောက်ရှိ subject နှင့် ညှိပါ
  • အထူးသတိပြုရန်မှာ either ဝါကျတွင် အငြင်းစကားလုံး မထည့်ရပါ
Extra info on either ... or

both ... and ကဲ့သို့ either ... or သည်လည်း အမျိုးအစားမတူသော စကားလုံးများကို ဆက်သွယ်နိုင်သည်

◾You can either have tea or coffee.
◾He's either in London or New York.
◾You'll either leave this house or I'll call the police.

▶ Click here for more information


Examples:
1. Nyi Nyi must have eaten the cake. If not, Ko Ko must have eaten it.
Either Nyi Nyi or Ko Ko must have eaten the cake.
2. You can fill in the form yourself. Or you can ask the clerk to do it for you.
You can either fill in the form yourself or ask the clerk to do it for you.
3. If you don't go now, I will call the police.
Either you go now or I will call the police.
Exercises
1. Mya Mya may be with Papa in her study. She may be with May May in the kitchen.

▶ Mya Mya may be either .....

Mya Mya may be either with Papa in her study or with May May in the kitchen.

2. Cho Cho sometimes comes here. Her sisters sometimes come here.

▶ Either .....

Either Cho Cho or her sisters sometimes come here.

3. Mi Mi is coming to the party. Her sisters are coming to the party.

▶ Either .....

Either Mi Mi or her sisters are coming to the party.

4. Mother cooks something special for us at weekends. If not, she takes us to a restaurant.

▶ Mother .....

Mother either cooks something special for us or takes us to a restaurant at weekends.

5. The motor can be fed by an electric current. The motor can be fed by a battery.

▶ The motor can be fed either .....

The motor can be fed either by an electric current or by a battery.

6. If you don't sit quietly, you'll have to leave the room.

▶ Either .....

Either you sit quietly or you'll have to leave the room.

7. The laser is used in surgical operations. If not, the knife is used in surgical operations.

▶ Either .....

Either the laser or the knife is used in surgical operations.

8. Cho Cho may have come here or Nyo Nyo may have come here.

▶ Either .....

Either Cho Cho or Nyo Nyo may have come here.

9. You can sit here or you can wait outside.

▶ You can either .....

You can either sit here or wait outside.

10. You are right. If not, I am right.

▶ Either .....

Either you or I am right.
