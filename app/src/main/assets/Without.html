Without


  • တစ်စုံတစ်ခုကိုမလုပ်ဘဲ... နောက်တစ်ခုလုပ်သည် ဟူသော အခြေအနေကို ဖေါ်ပြလိုသောအခါတွင် without ကို အသုံးပြုသည်
  • စာမကျက်ဘဲ အိပ်ယာစောဝင်သည်, အမေ့ကိုမပြောဘဲ သူငယ်ချင်းတွေနဲ့ သွားလည်သည် စသည့် ပုံစံများဖြစ်သည်
  • ဝါကျနှစ်ကြောင်းကို without ဖြင့် ဆက်ရာတွင် အငြင်းစကားလုံး ပါသောဝါကျမှ verb ကို -ing ပုံစံပြောင်းပြီး without နောက်တွင်ရေးပါ
  • အငြင်းစကားလုံးပါသောဝါကျဆုံးလျင် comma ( , ) ခံပြီး ကျန်ဝါကျကို ဆက်ကူးပါ
  • ဝါကျကို if/ unless ဖြင့် ဆက်ထားလျင် if/ unless ပါသောဝါကျမှ verb ကို -ing ပုံစံ ပြောင်းပေးရသည်
  • ဝါကျတွင် no one/ nobody ပါလျင် without + being + past participle (V3) ပုံစံ ရေးပေးရသည်... no one/ nobody နှင့် him, her, them တို့ကို ဖြုတ်ပါ
  • ဝါကျရှိ subject များမတူလျင် ( သို့ ) verb သည် be + past participle (V3) ပုံစံ ဖြစ်လျင်လည်း without + being + past participle (V3) ပုံစံ ရေးပေးရသည်
Examples:

1. Tun Tun went to bed early. He did not study his lessons.
Without studying his lessons, Tun Tun went to bed early.
2. You will not pass the exam if you don't try hard.
Without trying hard, you will not pass the exam.
3. You cannot get a distinction in English unless you practise letters and essays.
Without practising letters and essays, you cannot get a distinction in English.
4. Maung Maung went into the office. No one saw him.
Without being seen, Maung Maung went into the office.
5. He left the party. Nobody heard him.
Without being heard, he left the party.
6. The girl cried. She was not beaten.
Without being beaten, the girl cried.
7. She will not come to your birthday party if you don't invite her.
Without being invited, she will not come to your birthday party.
Exercises
1. Aung Aung passed the exam. He did not work hard.

▶ Without .....

Without working hard, Aung Aung passed the exam.

2. Ni Ni laid the table for dinner. Nobody told her.

▶ Without .....

Without being told, Ni Ni laid the table for dinner.

3. My sister went to the market. She did not take any money with her.

▶ Without .....

Without taking any money with her, my sister went to the market.

4. This dog could do some tricks. Nobody trained him.

▶ Without .....

Without being trained, this dog could do some tricks.

5. Don't expect to get high marks if you don' t study your lessons regularly.

▶ Without .....

Without studying your lessons regularly, don't expect to get high marks.

6. He repaired the tap. No one reminded him.

▶ Without .....

Without being reminded, he repaired the tap.

7. Mya Mya attended the party. She was not invited.

▶ Without .....

Without being invited, Mya Mya attended the party.

8. A stranger entered the room. Nobody noticed him.

▶ Without .....

Without being noticed, a stranger entered the room.

9. He had his meal. He did not wash his hands carefully.

▶ Without .....

Without washing his hands carefully, he had his meal.

10. He can swim well. He was not taught to swim.

▶ Without .....

Without being taught, he can swim well.
