Parallel Structure


  • Parallel structure သည် အကြောင်းအရာနှစ်ခု တစ်ပြိုင်တည်း ပြောင်းလဲနေခြင်းကို ဖော်ပြသည်
  • အောင်မြင်လေ မာနကြီးလေ, စာမေးပွဲနီးလာလေ ပိုစိတ်လှုပ်ရှားလေ စသည့် ပုံစံများဖြစ်သည်
  • ဝါကျတွင် comparative degree ပါလျင် the နှင့်တွဲပြီး ဝါကျအစတွင် ရေးပါ
  • positive degree ပါလျင် comparative degree ပြောင်းပါ
  • ၎င်းနောက်တွင် Subject နှင့် Verb ကို ဆက်ရေးပါ
  • Subject သည် Noun ဖြစ်လျင် is/ are ကို ဖြုတ်ပါ
  • as, when, if, and စသည့် စကားဆက်များ ဖြုတ်ပါ
  • ဝါကျတွင် အငြင်းစကားလုံးပါလျင် the less သို့ပြောင်းပါ
  • more နောက်တွင် Noun တွဲထားလျင် The more + Noun ပုံစံ ရေးပေးရသည်
Examples:
1. As they became richer, they spent more and more.
The richer they became, the more they spent.
2. Nilar became successful and she was proud.
The more successful Nilar became, the prouder she was.
3. When the sun is high, the day is hot.
The higher the sun, the hotter the day.
4. If you don't smoke a lot, it is good for your health.
The less you smoke, the better it is for your health.
5. He became more excited and he made more mistakes.
The more excited he became, the more mistakes he made.
Exercises
1. If the river is deeper, the danger is greater.

▶ The deeper .....

The deeper the river, the greater the danger.

2. He worked hard. He earned more money.

▶ The harder .....

The harder he worked, the more money he earned.

3. When the goods are scarce, the demand for them is great.

▶ The scarcer .....

The scarcer the goods, the greater the demand for them.

4. When she becomes angry, she speaks louder.

▶ The angrier .....

The angrier she becomes, the louder she speaks.

5. As she grew old, she became patient.

▶ The older .....

The older she grew, the more patient she became.

6. If the wind is strong, the waves are big.

▶ The stronger .....

The stronger the wind, the bigger the waves.

7. If the position is high, the responsibility is heavy.

▶ The higher .....

The higher the position, the heavier the responsibility.

8. If the sentence is short, I understand well.

▶ The shorter .....

The shorter the sentence, the better I understand.

9. The air gets thin as we climb high.

▶ The higher .....

The higher we climb, the thinner the air gets.

10. As she thought more about her health, she became more depressed.

▶ The more .....

The more she thought about her health, the more depressed she became.
