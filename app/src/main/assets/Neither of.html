Neither of ...


  • Neither ... nor နှင့်မတူသည်က Neither of... သည် ဝါကျနှစ်ကြောင်းကို မဆက်သွယ်ပေးပါ
  • Neither of + Noun သည် ဝါကျတစ်ကြောင်း၏ Subject ( သို့ ) Object သာ ဖြစ်နိုင်သည်
  • Neither of... နောက်ရှိ Noun သည် အများကိန်းဖြစ်ပြီး Verb သည်တစ်ခုကိန်းဖြစ်ရသည်
  • ဝါကျတွင် my sister/ brother and I ပါလျင် Neither of us သို့ ပြောင်းပါ
  • He/She and his sister/brother တို့ပါလျင် Neither of them သို့ ပြောင်းပါ
  • Neither of ဝါကျတွင်အငြင်းစကားလုံးမပါရပါ
  • စကားဆက်များဖြုတ်ပါ....
Examples:
1. Both sisters do not have long hair.
Neither of the sisters has long hair.
2. He and his brother did not go to the party last night.
Neither of them went to the party last night.
3. My sister and I are not going on a trip this summer.
Neither of us is going on a trip this summer.
4. Both the brothers do not want to attend computer courses.
Neither of the brothers wants to attend computer courses.
Exercises
1. I don't like either of the blouses you've bought.

▶ I like .....

I like neither of the blouses you've bought.

2. Both girls are not punctual.

▶ Neither of .....

Neither of the girls is punctual.

3. The twin brothers did not pay attention to their studies.

▶ Neither of .....

Neither of the brothers paid attention to his studies.

4. My sister and I cannot answer this question.

▶ Neither of .....

Neither of us can answer this question.

5. Both the boys are not to blame.

▶ Neither of .....

Neither of the boys is to be blamed.

6. Both the two girls are not attractive.

▶ Neither of .....

Neither of the girls is attractive.

7. My father was not born in Mandalay. My mother was not born in Mandalay, either.

▶ Neither of .....

Neither of my parents was born in Mandalay.

8. The first story is not interesting and neither is the second.

▶ Neither of .....

Neither of the stories is interesting.

9. The two handsets are not cheap.

▶ Neither of .....

Neither of the handsets is cheap.

10. Both the workers do not impress me much.

▶ Neither of .....

Neither of the workers impresses me much.
