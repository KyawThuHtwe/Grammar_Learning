Active ⇒ Passive


Introduction

  • ဝါကျတစ်ကြောင်းရှိ subject သည် လုပ်ဆောင်မှု တစ်ခုကို ပြုလုပ်သူ (doer of the action) ဖြစ်လျင် active verb form ကိုအသုံးပြုသည်
  • ဝါကျတစ်ကြောင်းရှိ subject သည် လုပ်ဆောင်မှု တစ်ခုကိုခံရသူ (receiver of the action) ဖြစ်လျင် passive verb form ကိုအသုံးပြုသည်
Active voice and passive voice in action

◾The man killed the snake.

The man သည် killed (သတ်သည်) ဟူသော လုပ်ဆောင်မှုကို ပြုလုပ်သူ ဖြစ်ပြီး the snake သည် ထိုလုပ်ဆောင်မှုကို ခံရသူဖြစ်သည်
The man killed ⇢ the snake.

◾The man was bitten by the snake.

The man သည် bite (ကိုက်သည်) ဟူသော လုပ်ဆောင်မှုကို ခံရသူဖြစ်ပြီး the snake သည် ထိုလုပ်ဆောင်မှုကို ပြုလုပ်သူဖြစ်သည်... passive ဝါကျတွင် ပြုလုပ်သူကို agent ဟု ခေါ်သည်
The man ⇠ was bitten by the snake.


Passive verb forms

  • verb သည် be + past participle (V3) ပုံစံဖြစ်လျင် passive verb form ဖြစ်သည်
  • အထူးသတိပြုရန်မှာ verb to be ပါတိုင်း (သို့) past participle (V3) ပါတိုင်း passive verb form မဟုတ်ပါ
  • verb to be နှင့် past participle (V3) နှစ်ခုလုံးပါမှ passive verb form ဖြစ်သည်
  • passive verb form တွင် tense (ကာလ) အားလုံးတွင် past participle (V3) ဖြင့် အဆုံးသတ်သည်ကို တွေ့ရှိနိုင်သည်
active verb form နှင့် passive verb form
ခွဲခြားခြင်း

◾He was calling.
verb to be (was) ပါသော်လည်း past participle (V3) မပါသောကြောင့် active verb form (past progressive tense) ဖြစ်သည်

◾He was called.
verb to be (was) နှင့် past participle (V3) နှစ်ခုလုံးပါသောကြောင့် passive verb form (simple past tense) ဖြစ်သည်
◾He has called.
past participle (V3) ပါသော်လည်း verb to be မပါသောကြောင့် active verb form (present perfect tense) ဖြစ်သည်

  • active ဝါကျမှ passive ဝါကျသို့ ပြောင်းလဲရာတွင် subject နှင့် object နေရာချင်းပြောင်းပါ
  • verb ကို ဇယားအတိုင်းပြောင်းပါ
  • active ဝါကျမှ subject ကို by ခံပြီး agent အဖြစ်ပြောင်းပါ
  • active ဝါကျရှိ subject သည် people, someone, somebody, no one, nobody စသည့် မသေချာသည့် subject များဖြစ်လျင် (သို့) I, we, you, he, she, they စသည့် နာမ်စားများဖြစ်လျင် ၎င်းတို့ကို by ခံပြီး ပြန်ရေးစရာ မလိုပါ
  • ဝါကျတွင် adverb phrases of place (နေရာပြ ကြိယာဝိသေသန စကားစုများ) ပါလျင် ၎င်းတို့ကို passive ဝါကျတွင် verb နောက် ကပ်ရေးပေးရသည်
    e.g. on the table, at the station, to Yangon
  • ဝါကျတွင် adverb phrases of time (အချိန်ပြ ကြိယာဝိသေသနများ) ပါလျင် ၎င်းတို့ကို passive ဝါကျတွင် နောက်ဆုံးမှ ရေးရသည်
    e.g. at weekends, on Sunday, when I arrived home
  • for နှင့် တွဲထားသော စကားစုများသည် verb နောက် ကပ်ရေးနိုင်သလို နောက်ဆုံးတွင်လည်း ရေးနိုင်သည်

Examples:
1. My grandfather built this house.
This house was built by my grandfather.
2. Nilar is watering the plants.
The plants are being watered by Nilar.
3. Ko Ko has done the homework.
The homework has been done by Ko Ko.
4. People play football all over the world.
Football is played all over the world.
5. Someone broke the windows last night.
The windows were broken last night.
6. No one can solve this problem.
This problem cannot be solved.
7. They are digging a well in the garden.
A well is being dug in the garden.
8. He is going to tidy up his room.
His room is going to be tidied up.
9. We completed the project well ahead of time.
The project was completed well ahead of time.
10. Daw Nilar is going to send her son to a boarding school on Monday.
Her son is going to be sent to a boarding school by Daw Nilar on Monday.
11. The police found the stolen jewels under the bed.
The stolen jewels were found under the bed by the police.
12. The villagers caught and killed the man-eating tiger yesterday.
The man-eating tiger was caught and killed by the villagers yesterday.
13. The teacher beat him for not doing the homework.
He was beaten by the teacher for not doing the homework.
(or)
He was beaten for not doing the homework by the teacher.

Passive ⇒ Active

  • passive ဝါကျမှ by ခံထားသော agent ကို subject နေရာတွင်ရေးပါ
  • verb ကို ဇယားအတိုင်းပြောင်းပါ
  • verb နောက်တွင် passive ဝါကျမှ subject ကိုရေးပါ
  • ကျန်စကားစုများ ဆက်ရေးပါ
  • ဝါကျတွင် by + agent မပါလျင် သင့်တော်ရာ subject တစ်ခု ထည့်ပေးရသည်
Examples:
1. Tun Tun was awarded the first prize by the headmaster.
The headmaster awarded Tun Tun the first prize.
2. Min Min will be met at the station by his friend tomorrow.
His friend will meet Min Min at the station tomorrow.
3. These paintings must not be touched.
No one must touch these paintings.
4. The books were being arranged on the shelf by Ma Ma when I called her.
Ma Ma was arranging the books on the shelf when I called her.
5. A bridge has been built across the river.
They have built a bridge across the river.

Questions

  • အမေးဝါကျများကို active မှ passive သို့ အပြန်အလှန် ပြောင်းလဲရာတွင် subject ရှေ့တွင် အမြဲတမ်း အကူကြိယာ ရှိရသည်
  • question mark ( ? ) ပြန်ထည့်ပေးရသည်ကို သတိပြုပါ
  • ကျန်ပြောင်းလဲပုံများမှာ အတူတူပင်ဖြစ်သည်
Examples:
1. Did Ko Ko return the books to the library yesterday?
Were the books returned to the library by Ko Ko yesterday?
2. Is Nilar washing the clothes now?
Are the clothes being washed by Nilar now?
3. Have they repaired the broken window?
Has the broken window been repaired?
4. Will the teacher punish them tomorrow?
Will they be punished by the teacher tomorrow?
5. Is your father going to buy a computer for you?
Is a computer going to be bought for you by your father?
6. Where will mother take us this summer?
Where will we be taken by mother this summer?
7. How do they make computers?
How are computers made?
8. Why did Thein Tun take the laptop in the office?
Why was the laptop in the office taken by Thein Tun?
9. Who is making that noise?
By whom is that noise being made?
10. What did scientists do about that problem?
What was done by scientist about that problem?
11. Were the drugs burned by the police after the seizure?
Did the police burn the drugs after the seizure?
12. Were the dishes being washed by Ni Ni when you arrived?
Was Ni Ni washing the dishes when you arrived?
13. Are the books in the library regularly dusted?
Do they dust the books in the library regularly?
14. When are those buildings going to be torn down?
When are they going to tear those buildings down?
15. By whom have the flowers been picked?
Who has picked the flowers?
Exercises
1. U Kaung drove the car recklessly.

▶ The car .....

The car was recklessly driven by U Kaung.

2. The clothes have been washed by the maid.

▶ The maid .....

The maid has washed the clothes.

3. They did not inform me about it.

▶ I .....

I was not informed about it.

4. Doctors have not found out the cause of his illness yet.

▶ The cause of his illness .....

The cause of his illness has not been found out by doctors yet.

5. She spent all her money on clothes.

▶ All her money .....

All her money was spent on clothes.

6. We cannot see bacteria without a microscope.

▶ Bacteria .....

Bacteria cannot be seen without a microscope.

7. My cook always makes a number of tasty dishes.

▶ A number of tasty dishes .....

A number of tasty dishes are always made by my cook.

8. My uncle is going to buy a house with a large compound.

▶ A house with a large compound .....

A house with a large compound is going to be bought by my uncle.

9. The winning goal was scored by Aung Aung.

▶ Aung Aung .....

Aung Aung scored the winning goal.

10. The maid arranged the flowers in the vase beautifully.

▶ The flowers .....

The flowers were beautifully arranged in the vase by the maid.

11. Nobody must take these books from the library.

▶ These books .....

These books must not be taken away from the library.

12. Aunty Chaw gives us lots of presents every New Year.

▶ We .....

We are given lots of presents by Aunty Chaw every New Year.

13. She tore the letter into pieces.

▶ The letter .....

The letter was torn into pieces.

14. Why have they moved your table?

▶ Why .....

Why has your table been moved?

15. Who sent you this email?

▶ By whom .....

By whom were you sent this email?

16.Who will take care of the baby?

▶ By whom .....

By whom will the baby be taken care of?

17. Somebody left the lights on all night.

▶ The lights .....

The lights were left on all night.

18. Did someone use my computer in my absence?

▶ Was .....

Was my computer used in my absence?

19. A new engine is being replaced in my car by the mechanic.

▶ The mechanic .....

The mechanic is replacing a new engine in my car.

20. His father beat him for not attending classes regularly.

▶ He .....

He was beaten by his father for not attending classes regularly.

21. A group of scientists are carrying out an experiment systematically.

▶ An experiment .....

An experiment is being systematically carried out by a group of scientists.

22. Nothing is known about the past.

▶ No one .....

No one knows about the past.

23. When glass was invented, the hourglass came into use.

▶ When people .....

When people invented glass, the hourglass came into use.

24. They didn't tell me anything about the matter.

▶ I .....

I wasn't told anything about the matter.

25. Was the windowpane broken by the children?

▶ Did .....

Did the children break the windowpane?

26. More fuel is used by old cars than new cars.

▶ Old cars .....

Old cars use more fuel than new cars.

27. Where did Maung Maung keep the money?

▶ Where .....

Where was the money kept by Maung Maung?

28. Who is using the computer now?

▶ By whom .....

By whom is the computer being used now?

29. The flood destroyed many cars in Thailand.

▶ Many cars in Thailand .....

Many cars in Thailand were destroyed by the flood.

30. I was taught by experience that life is meaningful.

▶ Experience .....

Experience taught me that life is meaningful.
