Nouns in Apposition


  • Noun တစ်ခု၏ နောက်တွင်နေပြီး ယင်း Noun ကို ထပ်ဖြည့်ဖော်ပြသည့် Noun (or) Noun group ကို apposition ဟုခေါ်သည်
  • ဝါကျနှစ်ကြောင်းရှိ အမျိုးအစားတူ Noun နှစ်ခုကို comma( , ) ခံပြီး ရေးပေးရသည်
  • ဒုတိယဝါကျမှ Sub + be ကိုဖြုတ်ပြီး ကျန် Noun စကားစုကို ရှေ့ဝါကျမှ သက်ဆိုင်ရာ Noun နောက်တွင် comma( , ) ခံပြီး ရေးပေးရသည်
  • တစ်ခါတစ်ရံတွင် Noun နှစ်ခုလုံး ရှေ့ဝါကျတွင် ရှိတတ်သည်
  • အထူးသတိပြုရန်မှာ Nouns in apposition သည် အမျိုးအစားတူ Noun နှစ်ခုကို အတူရေးခြင်းဖြစ်သည်... Noun + Noun သာဖြစ်ရသည်.... Noun + Adj/ Noun + Verb/ Noun + Adv ဘယ်တော့မှ မဖြစ်ရပါ
Examples:
1. We all love Daw Nilar. She is our class teacher.
We all love Daw Nilar , our class teacher.
2. Phyu Phyu always stands first in class. She is my best friend.
Phyu Phyu, my best friend , always stands first in class.
3. We are going to Bagan this summer. Bagan is an ancient city.
We are going to Bagan, an ancient city , this summer.
4. Mogok is my native town. It is famous for its rubies.
Mogok , my native town , is famous for its rubies.
5. Everest is the highest mountain in the world. It is situated in Nepal.
Everest, the highest mountain in the world, is situated in Nepal.
Exercises
1. Inlay lake is surrounded by hazy bue mountains. It is one of the famous resorts in Myanmar.

▶ Inlay Lake, .....

Inlay Lake, one of the famous resorts in Myanmar, is surrounded by hazy blue mountains.

2. U Win has gone to England. He is the managing director of this company.

▶ U Win, .....

U Win, the managing director of this company, has gone to England.

3. Are you interested in the Chinese language?.It is one of the most difficult languages to learn.

▶ Are you interested .....

Are you interested in the Chinese language, one of the most difficult languages to learn?

4. The Ayeyarwady flows from north to south. It is the most useful river in Myanmar.

▶ The Ayeyarwady, .....

The Ayeyarwady, the most useful river in Myanmar, flows from north to south.

5. Swe Swe is very talkative. She is one of my cousins.

▶ Swe Swe, .....

Swe Swe, one of my cousins, is very talkative.

6. Ko Ba Chit looks after the plants in our garden. He is our cook's husband.

▶ Ko Ba Chit, .....

Ko Ba Chit, our cook's husband, looks after the plants in our garden.

7. Pyin Oo Lwin is famous for flowers. It is a pleasant town.

▶ Pyin Oo Lwin, .....

Pyin Oo Lwin, a pleasant town, is famous for flowers.

8. Tobacco contains nicotine. Nicotine is a substance that stimulates the heart.

▶ Tobacco contains nicotine, .....

Tobacco contains nicotine, a substance that stimulates the heart.

9. I met Thida and Nilar in the market. Thida is my neighbour. Nilar is my cousin.

▶ I met .....

I met Nilar, my neighbour, and Thida, my cousin, in the market.

10. Mount Popa lies 50 kilometers southeast of Bagan. Mount Popa is an extinct volcano.

▶ Mount Popa .....

Mount Popa, an extinct volcano, lies 50 kilometers southeast of Bagan.
