Although ⇄ In spite of


  • although/ in spite of သည် ဖော်ပြချက်နှစ်ခုတွင် အချက်တစ်ချက်သည် နောက်တစ်ချက်နှင့် ဆန့်ကျင်နေသည်ကို ဖော်ပြသည်
  • ဆင်းရဲသော်လည်း ရိုးသားသည်, ကြိုးစားသော်လည်း စာမေးပွဲ မအောင်ခဲ့ စသည့် ပုံစံများဖြစ်သည်
  • although ပါသော ဝါကျများကို in spite of သို့ ပြောင်းလဲ ရာတွင် ဝါကျနှစ်ခုရှိ subject များ တူမတူကြည့်ပါ
  • although ဝါကျရှိ subject နှင့် နောက်ဝါကျရှိ subject တူလျင် in spite of နောက်တွင် subject မထည့်ရပါ
  • ဝါကျရှိ subject များ မတူလျင် in spite of နောက်တွင် ရှေ့ဝါကျမှ subject ထည့်ပေးရသည်
  • verb ကို -ing ပုံစံပြောင်းပြီး ကျန်သည့်အပိုင်း ဆက်ကူးပါ
  • although ဝါကျတွင် အငြင်းစကားလုံးပါလျင် in spite of ဝါကျတွင် အငြင်းစကားလုံးကို V-ing ရှေ့ ရေးပေးရသည်
  • ရာသီဥတုနှင့်ဆိုင်သော စကားလုံးများကို noun form ဖြင့် အရေးများသည်ကို သတိပြုပါ
  • in spite of မှ although သို့ ပြောင်းရာတွင် although နောက်တွင် အမြဲတမ်း subject ရှိရသည်
  • although ဝါကျရှိ verb ကို နောက်ဝါကျရှိ verb နှင့် ညှိပါ
In spite of with pronouns

in spite of နောက်တွင် နာမ်စား (pronoun) ရှိလျင် ၎င်းနာမ်စားသည် ကံပုဒ် (object) (သို့) ပိုင်ဆိုင်မှုပြ (possessive) ပုံစံဖြစ်ရသည်

အောက်ပါ ဥပမာဝါကျများကို လေ့လာကြည့်ပါ

◾Although he tried very hard, the teacher was not pleased.
◾In spite of him/his trying very hard, the teacher was not pleased. (NOT ... In spite of he trying very hard ...)

◾Although I told the truth, no one believed me.
◾In spite of me/my telling the truth, no one believed me.
(NOT ... In spite of I telling ...)



Examples:
1. Although my sister took the medicine, she did not feel better.
In spite of taking the medicine, my sister did not feel better.
2. She is not proud although she is rich.
In spite of being rich, she is not proud.
3. Although Tun Tun got three distinctions, his father was not pleased.
In spite of Tun Tun getting three distinctions, his father was not pleased.
4. Although it was raining, he went out.
In spite of the rain, he went out.
5. Although it was raining heavily, the children were playing football.
In spite of the heavy rain, the children were playing football.
6. Although they do not have a lot of money, they are happy.
In spite of not having a lot of money, they are happy.
7. In spite of earning a lot of money, Ni Ni still can't buy a car.
Although Ni Ni earns a lot of money, she still can't buy a car.
8. In spite of falling off the ladder, he was not badly hurt.
Although he fell off the ladder, he was not badly hurt.
9. In spite of Naing Naing telling the truth, the police did not believe him.
Although Naing Naing told the truth, the police did not believe him.
Exercises
1. Although Kaung Myat was ill, he went to school to take the test.

▶ In spite of .....

In spite of being ill, Kaung Myat went to school to take the test.

2. Although Thida was excited, she was able to finish her speech.

▶ In spite of .....

In spite of being excited, Thida was able to finish her speech.

3. I am not feeling well although I take the prescribed medicine regularly.

▶ In spite of .....

In spite of taking the prescribed medicine regularly, I am not feeling well.

4. Although U Mya works very hard, his business is not thriving.

▶ In spite of .....

In spite of U Mya working very hard, his business is thriving.

5. Although plastic bags are cheap and useful, they are harmful to the environment.

▶ In spite of .....

In spite of being cheap and useful, plastic bags are harmful to the environment.

6. Although he had many degrees, he had trouble finding a job.

▶ In spite of .....

In spite of having many degrees, he had trouble finding a job.

7. Although Ko Ko fell off the ladder, he didn't break any bones.

▶ In spite of .....

In spite of falling off the ladder, he didn't break any bones.

8. In spite of being very rich, he never looks after his relatives.

▶ Although .....

Although he is very rich, he never looks after his relatives.

9. In spite of the questions being easy, few candidates did well in the exam.

▶ Although .....

Although the questions were easy, few candidates did well in the exam.

10. In spite of finding Physics boring, Kyi Pyar is good at it.

▶ Although .....

Although Kyi Pyar finds Physics boring, she is good at it.
