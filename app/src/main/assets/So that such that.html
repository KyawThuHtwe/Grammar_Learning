so ... that ⇄ such ... that


  • so ... that နှင့် such ... that မှာ အဓိပ္ပါယ် အတူတူပင်ဖြစ်သည်
  • ဝါကျတည်ဆောက်ပုံ အနည်းငယ်သာ ကွဲပြားသည်
  • so + adj + that ကို such + a/an + adj + that ပုံစံပြာင်းပြီး ကျန်သည့်အပိုင်း ဆက်ကူးပါ...
    much/ many ပါလျင် a lot of သို့ပြောင်းပါ....
  • such + a/an + adj + Noun + that ကို so + adj + that ပုံစံပြောင်းပြီး ကျန်သည့်အပိုင်း ဆက်ကူးပါ...
Examples:
1. Arkar is so lazy that he never does any work.
Arkar is such a lazy boy that he never does any work.
2. Thandar is so beautiful that she has a lot of admirers.
Thandar is such a beautiful girl that she has a lot of admirers.
3. The day was so cold that we did not want to go out.
It was such a cold day that we did not want to go out.
4. The knife is so sharp that it can cut through the meat easily.
It is such a sharp knife it can cut through the meat easily.
5. U Ba has so much money he can buy whatever he likes.
U Ba has such a lot of money that he can buy whatever he likes.
6. The question was so easy that I could answer it in no time at all.
It was such an easy question that I could answer it in no time at all.
7. I have collected so many books that my bookshelf cannot hold all of them.
I have collected such a lot of books that my bookshelf cannot hold all of them.
8. Daw Nilar is such a kind lady that all her neighbours love her.
Daw Nilar is so kind all her neighbours love her.
9. It was such a violent storm that many houses were destroyed.
The storm was so violent that many houses were destroyed.
Exercises
1. The night was so dark that there were few people on the road.

▶ It was such .....

It was such a dark night that there were few people on the road.

2. The lesson was so difficult that nobody understood it.

▶ It was such .....

It was such a difficult lesson that nobody understood it.

3. She is such a skinny girl that her parents are worried about her health.

▶ She is so .....

She is so skinny that her parents are worried about her health.

4. He is such a stingy person that nobody likes him.

▶ He is so .....

He is so stingy that nobody likes him.

5. It was such a frightening dream that I stayed awake for a long time.

▶ The dream was .....

The dream was so frightening that I stayed awake for a long time.

6. The earthquake was so strong that it caused a lot of damage near its epicenter.

▶ It was such .....

It wassuch a strong earthquake that it caused a lot of damage near its epicenter.

7. The man was so rich that he made a generous donation to the charity.

▶ He was such .....

He was such a rich man that he made a generous donation to the charity.

8. The book was so interesting that he did not want to put it down.

▶ It was such .....

It was such an interesting book that he did not want to put it down.

9. Han Zaw Tun is so obedient that every teacher likes him.

▶ Han Zaw Tun is such .....

Han Zaw Tun is such an obedient student that every teacher likes him.

10. The speech was so boring that everyone fell asleep.

▶ It was such .....

It was such a boring speech that everyone fell asleep.
