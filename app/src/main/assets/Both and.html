Both ... and


  • both ... and သည် ဝါကျနှစ်ကြောင်းရှိ အမျိုးအစားတူ စကားလုံး/ စကားစုနှစ်ခုကို ဆက်သွယ်ပေးသည်
  • အထူးသတိပြုရန်မှာ subject များကို both ... and ဖြင့် ဆက်ထားလျင် verb သည် အမြဲတမ်း အများကိန်းဖြစ်ရသည်
  • neither ... nor ဝါကျများကို both ... and သို့ ပြောင်းရာတွင် verb ကို အငြင်းပြောင်းပေးရသည်
  • ဝါကျရှိ and, too, also, as well စသည့် စကားဆက်များ ဖြုတ်ပေးရသည်
Extra info on both ... and

both ... and နောက်တွင် အမျိူးအစားတူ စကားလုံးများသာ လိုက်လေ့ရှိသည်
both + noun ... and + noun,
both + verb ... and + verb,
both + adjective ... and + adjective
စသည့် ပုံစံများဖြစ်သည်

◾She's both pretty and clever. (adjectives)
◾I spoke to both the Director and her secretary. (nouns)
◾She both dances and sings. (verbs)

အမျိုးအစားမတူသော စကားလုံးများကို ဆက်သွယ်ထားသော both ... and ဝါကျများလည်း တွေ့ရတတ်သည်

◾She both dances and she sings.
(both + verb; and + clause)
◾I both play the piano and the violin.
(both + clause; and + noun)

Examples:
1. Ma Ma gets up at 6 o 'clock every day. Ni Ni also gets up at 6 o'clock every day.
Both Ma Ma and Ni Ni get up at 6 o'clock every day.
2. I was hungry when I got home. I was tired when I got home.
I was both hungry and tired when I got home.
3. Ko Ko is a hardworking student. Nilar is also a hardworking student.
Both Ko Ko and Nilar are hardworking students.
4. Neither Kyi Pyar nor her brother attends classes regularly.
Both Kyi Pyar and her brother do not attend classes regularly.
Exercises
1. Not only Myat Myat but also Latt Latt is expert at using the computer.

▶ Both .....

Both Myat Myat and Latt Latt are expert at using the computer.

2. Soe Soe likes Korean films and so does Toe Toe.

▶ Both .....

Both Soe Soe and Toe Toe like Korean films.

3. U Than Toe is a good-natured doctor and so is Daw Mya Sein.

▶ Both .....

Both U Than Toe and Daw Mya Sein are good-natured doctors.

4. Tin Tin Mya is a famous vocalist. Cho Pyone is a famous vocalist.

▶ Both .....

Both Tin Tin Mya and Cho Pyone are famous vocalists.

5. Neither Ko Ko nor Nyi Nyi watched TV last night.

▶ Both .....

Both Ko Ko and Nyi Nyi did not watch TV last night.

6. He has no manners at all. His sister has no manners at all.

▶ Both .....

Both he and his sister have no manners at all.

7. Physics is a science subject. Chemistry is a science subject.

▶ Both .....

Both Physics and Chemistry are science subjects.

8. Not only the worm but also the snail does not have a backbone.

▶ Both .....

Both the worm and the snail do not have backbones.

9. The computer makes our lives convenient. The telephone makes our lives convenient.

▶ Both .....

Both the computer and the telephone make our lives better.

10. "Robinson Crusoe" is an adventure story. "Jungle Book" is an adventure story, too.

▶ Both .....

Both "Robinson Crusoe" and "Jungle Book" are adventure stories.
