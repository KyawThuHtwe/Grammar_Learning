Relative Clauses


  • ပေးထားသော ဝါကျနှစ်ကြောင်းမှ တူရာ Noun များကို who, whom, that, which တို့ဖြင့် ဆက်ပေးရသည်
    ▶ Click here for more information
  • လူကို who နှင့် ဆက်ပြီး အရာဝတ္ထုများကို that/ which နှင့် ဆက်သည်
  • ဝါကျကို who, whom နှင့် ဆက်လျင် နောက်ဝါကျမှ he, him ကို ဖြုတ်ပေးရသည်... ဝါကျကို that, which နှင့် ဆက်လျင် နောက်ဝါကျမှ it ကို ဖြုတ်ပေးရသည်
relatives or apposition ?

◾I want to visit Mogok. It is a beautiful hill station.

ယခုဝါကျကို နှစ်မျိုးပြောင်းနိုင်သည်... non-defining relative clause ပုံစံဖြစ်စေ nouns in apposition ပုံစံဖြစ်စေ ပြောင်းရေးနိုင်သည်

◾I want to visit Mogok, which is a beautiful hill station.
◾I want to visit Mogok, a beautiful hill station.

ယခုဝါကျများကို လေ့လာကြည့်လျင် nouns in apposition သည် non-defining relative clause နှင့် သဘောတရား အတူတူဖြစ်သည်ကို တွေ့ရမည်... ၎င်းတို့သည် ကျွန်တော်တို့ ပြောဆိုနေသော လူ/ အရာဝတ္ထု အကြောင်းကို ထပ်ဆောင်းပြောပြသည်... သို့သော် non-defining relative clause များ အားလုံးကို nouns in apposition သို့ ပြောင်းရေးနိုင်မည် မဟုတ်ပါ

◾I want to visit Mogok. It is surrounded on all sides by mountains.
◾I want to visit Mogok, which is surrounded on all sides by mountains.
( NOT I want to visit Mogok, is surrounded ...)

ယခုဝါကျကို non-defining relative clause ပုံစံ ရေးနိုင်သော်လည်း nouns in apposition ပုံစံတော့ ရေးလို့မရနိုင်ပါ... nouns in apposition ဆိုသည်မှာ noun နှစ်ခုကို အတူတွဲရေးခြင်း ဖြစ်သည်


Examples:
1. We all love Daw Nilar. She is our class teacher.
We all love Daw Nilar, who is our class teacher.
2. My laptop is expensive. I bought it from Singapore.
My laptop, which I bought from Singapore, is expensive.
3. Do you know the man? He is sitting on a bench in the garden.
Do you know the man who is sitting on the bench in the garden?
4. My teacher gave me a book. Its covers were missing.
My teacher gave me a book whose covers were missing.
5. Many people attempt to climb Mount Everest. It is the highest mountain in the world.
Many people attempt to climb Mount Everest, which is the highest mountain in the world.
Exercises
1. We all like Daw Mu Mu. She is a good teacher.

▶ We all .....

We all like Daw Mu Mu, who is a good teacher.

2. Our village pagoda has been rebuilt. It was destroyed by an earthquake.

▶ Our village pagoda, .....

Our village pagoda, which was destroyed by an earthquake, has been rebuilt.

3. I have lost my watch. I got it from my father as a birthday present.

▶ I have lost .....

I have lost my watch, which I got from my father as a birthday present.

4. Mogok is a pleasant town. I want to visit there.

▶ I want to visit .....

I want to visit Mogok, which is a pleasant town.

5. Her daughter was injured in a car accident. She is now in hospital.

▶ Her daughter, .....

Her daughter, who was injured in a car accident, is now in hospital.

6. The movie wasn't very good. We watched it last night.

▶ The movie which .....

The movie which we watched last night wasn't very good.

7. Mango is liked by almost all people in Myanmar. It is a tropical fruit.

▶ Mango, .....

Mango, which is a tropical fruit, is liked by almost all people in Myanmar.

8. Thura bought a watch from Ga Mone Pwint Store. It is for his only sister.

▶ The watch .....

The watch which Thura bought a watch from Ga Mone Pwint Store is for his only sister.

9. I lent him 'The Old Man and the Sea'. It was not so difficult to read.

▶ I lent .....

I lent him 'The Old Man and the Sea', which was not so difficult to read.

10. Here is the key. It opens the gate.

▶ Here is .....

Here is the key which opens the gate.
