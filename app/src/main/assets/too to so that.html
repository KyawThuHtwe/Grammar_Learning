too to ... so that


  • too ... to သည် တစ်စုံတစ်ခုကို လုပ်နိုင်လောက်အောင် အရည်အသွေး မရှိဟု အဓိပ္ပါယ်ရသည်
  • မေးခွန်းက ဖြေနိုင်ဖို့ခက်လွန်းတယ်, အပင်က တက်ဖို့ မြင့်လွန်းတယ် စသည့် ပုံစံများဖြစ်သည်
  • too ... to မှ so ... that သို့ပြောင်းရာတွင် too ကို ဖြုတ်ပြီး so + adjective + that ပုံစံရေးပါ
  • Subject များတူလျင် ရှေ့ဝါကျမှ Subject ကို that နောက်တွင် ရေးပေးရသည်
  • Subject များမတူလျင် for ခံထားသော နာမ်စားများကို that နောက်တွင် ရေးပေးရသည်
  • to ကိုဖြုတ်ပြီး cannot/ could not နှင့် ဆက်ပါ
  • ဝါကျရှိ Verb သည် present (V1) ဖြစ်လျင် cannot နှင့်ဆက်ပြီး past (V2) ဖြစ်လျင် could not နှင့် ဆက်ပေးရသည်
  • Subject မတူသော ဝါကျများကို ဆက်သွယ်ရာတွင် Verb နောက်တွင် ရှေ့ဝါကျမှ Subject ကို ညွှန်းသောစကားလုံးများ ပြန်ထည့်ပေးရသည်
too ... to and so ... that

too ... to သည် Simple sentence ဖြစ်ပြီး so ... that သည် Complex sentence ဖြစ်သည်

◾The tree is too high for him to climb.

ယခုဝါကျကို so ... that သို့ပြောင်းလျင် Subject နှစ်ခု မတူသည်ကို တွေ့ရမည်... The tree နှင့် he ဖြစ်သည်
"The tree is so high that he cannot climb." ဟုရေးလျင် climb နောက်တွင် Object တစ်ခု လိုနေသည်
climb သည် ရှေ့ဝါကျမှ tree ကို ညွှန်းဆိုနေသောကြောင့် tree ၏နာမ်စား it ထည့်ပေးရသည်
◾The tree is so high that he cannot climb it.✔

Some more examples :
◾The coffee is too hot for me to drink.
◾The coffee is so hot that I could not drink it.

◾The questions were too difficult for her to answer.
◾The questions were so difficult that she could not answer them.

too ... to သည် Simple sentence ဖြစ်ပြီး Subject နှင့် Verb to be တစ်ခုသာ ပါသောကြောင့် it/ them ပြန်ထည့်ပေးစရာ မလိုပါ



Examples:
1. She was too busy to have lunch.
She was so busy that she could not havelunch.
2. Thura is too short to become a basketball player.
Thura is so short that he cannot becomea basketball player.
3. The box is too heavy for her to carry.
The box is so heavy that she cannot carry it.
4. The tree was too high for them to climb.
The tree was so high that they could not climb it.
Exercises
1. Your skin is too delicate to be exposed to the sun directly.

▶ Your skin is so .....

Your skin is so delicate that it cannot be exposed to the sun directly.

2. He drove the car too fast to avoid the collision.

▶ He drove the car .....

He drove the car so fast that he could not avoid the collision.

3. We sometimes work too hard to enjoy life.

▶ We sometimes work so .....

We sometimes work so hard that we cannot enjoy life.

4. The shirt is too big to fit you.

▶ The shirt is so .....

The shirt is so big that it cannot fit you

5. He was so excited that he could not answer the questions well.

▶ He was too .....

He was too excited to answer the questions well.

6. She has changed so much that I can hardly recognize her.

▶ She has changed too .....

She has changed too much for me to recognize.

7. The print is so small that my grandmother cannot read it.

▶ The print is too .....

The print is too small for my grandmother to read.

8. The wall was so high that the children could not climb over it.

▶ The wall was too .....

The wall was too high for the children to climb over.
