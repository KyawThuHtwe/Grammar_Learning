When ... V-ing


  • ဝါကျကို as, when, because တို့ဖြင့် ဆက်ထားလျင် as, when, because ပါသောဝါကျမှ verb ကို -ing ပုံစံပြောင်းပြီး ဝါကျအစတွင် ရေးပေးရသည်
  • ဝါကျကို and, then, so တို့ဖြင့် ဆက်ထားလျင် ရှေ့ဝါကျမှ verb ကို -ing ပုံစံ ပြောင်းပေးရသည်
  • ဝါကျထဲရှိ စကားဆက်များ ဖြုတ်ပေးရသည်
  • ဝါကျဆုံးလျင် comma( , ) ခံပါ... comma( , ) နောက်တွင် အမြဲတမ်း subject ရှိရသည်
  • တစ်ခါတစ်ရံ ဝါကျတွင် past (V2) ပါပြီး having နှင့် စမေးထားလျင် have + past perfect (V3) ပုံစံ ရေးပေးရသည်

Sentences starting with V-ing

ဝါကျများကို V-ing (present participle) ဖြင့် စရေးထားခြင်းသည် အလုပ်တစ်ခုလုပ်ပြီး နောက်တစ်ခုလုပ်ခြင်း၊ အလုပ်တစ်ခုလုပ်နေစဥ် နောက်တစ်ခုကိုလုပ်ခြင်း၊ အကြောင်းပြချက်တို့ကို ဖော်ပြသည်
အောက်ပါ ဥပမာဝါကျများကိုလေ့လာကြည့်ပါ

◾Putting down my newspaper, I walked over to the window. (= After I had put down.....)
◾ Holding the hair-dryer in her left hand, Susan cut her hair with the scissors in her right hand. (= While Susan was holding ....)
◾ Being unable to help in any other way, I gave her some money. (= As I was unable to help ...)



Examples:
1. The thieves ran when they saw the policeman.
Seeing the policeman, the thieves ran away.
2. He took out his guitar. Then, he began to sing a song.
Taking out his guitar, he began to sing a son.
3. Nga Pyin never studies his lessons because he is very lazy.
Being very lazy, Nga Pyin never studies his lessons.
4. As I walked down the street, I bumped into a friend of mine.
Walking down the street, I bumped into a friend of mine.
5. He has a lot of money so he can buy anything he likes.
Having a lot of money, he can buy anything he likes.
6. Nilar did the housework and then relaxed in front of TV.
Having done the housework, Nilar relaxed in front of TV.
Exercises
1. Soe Soe apologized to Mee Mee when she realized her mistake.

▶ Realizing .....

Realizing her mistake, So Soe apologized to Mee Mee.

2. When I stood on top of the mountain, I could see the town clearly.

▶ Standing .....

Standing on top of the mountain, I could see the town clearly.

3. As the boy didn't know the answer to the question, he kept quiet.

▶ Not knowing .....

Not knowing the answer to the question, the boy kept quiet.

4. When she thought that she had worried her father, she was unhappy.

▶ Thinking .....

Thinking that she had worried her father, she was unhappy.

5. My brother rushed into the kitchen when he arrived home from school.

▶ Arriving .....

Arriving home from school, my brother rushed into the kitchen.

6. I had one delay after another. I am starting to get fed up.

▶ Having .....

Having one delay after another, I am starting to get fed up.

7. When the bus stopped suddenly, it tipped me out of my seat.

▶ Stopping .....

Stopping suddenly, the bus tipped me out of my seat.

8. When she was offered a good salary, she accepted the job.

▶ Being .....

Being offered a good salary, she accepted the job.

9. He had seen me in a photograph. So he recognized me at once.

▶ Having .....

Having seen me in a photograph, he recognized me at once.

10. Su Su did her homework. Then, she said her prayers and went to bed.

▶ Having .....

Having done her homework, Su Su said her prayers and went to bed.
