After


  • after သည် အလုပ်တစ်ခုကိုလုပ်ပြီး နောက်တစ်ခုကိုလုပ်သည့် အခြေအနေကို ဖော်ပြရာတွင်သုံးသည်
  • ပန်းပင်တွေရေလောင်းပြီးနောက် အနားယူတယ်, အလုပ်ပြီးတော့ အိမ်ပြန်တယ် စသည့် ပုံစံများဖြစ်သည်
  • ဝါကျနှစ်ကြောင်းကို after ဖြင့်ဆက်ရာတွင် ရှေ့ဝါကျမှ verb ကို -ing ပုံစံပြောင်းပြီး after နောက်တွင်ရေးပါ
  • ဝါကျဆုံးလျင် comma( , ) ခံပေးရသည်... comma( , ) နောက်တွင် အမြဲတမ်း subject ထည့်ပေးရသည်
  • ဝါကျရှိ and/then/and then/first တို့ကိုဖြုတ်ပါ
  • after နောက်တွင် subject ပေးထားလျင် verb ကို past perfect (had + V3) ပုံစံ ရေးပေးရသည်
  • ဝါကျကို when ဖြင့်ဆက်ထားလျင် when ပါသောဝါကျကို after ပြောင်းပေးရသည်
As soon as နဲ့ After က ဘာတွေကွာတာလဲ ?

as soon as နှင့် after တို့သည် အဖြစ်အပျက်/ လုပ်ဆောင်ချက်နှစ်ခု တစ်ခုပြီးတစ်ခု ဖြစ်ပေါ်ခြင်းကို ပြသည် ဆိုသော်လည်း as soon as က after ထက် အချိန်ပိုပြီး နီးကပ်သည်... as soon as သည် immediately after ဟု ဆိုလိုသည်

အောက်ပါ ဥပမာဝါကျများကို လေ့လာကြည့်ပါ

◾As soon as he got the money, he rushed out to buy an ice-cream.
( ပိုက်ဆံရတာနဲ့ အပြင်ကို တန်းပြေးထွက်ပုံ မြင်ယောင်နိုင်သည် )

◾She felt better after she had taken the medicine.
After သည် အချိန်အားဖြင့် As soon as လောက်နီးကပ်ခြင်းမရှိသည်ကို လေ့လာတွေ့ရှိနိုင်သည်... as soon as she had taken the medicine ဟု ဆိုလျင်တော့ အခုဆေးသောက် အခုပျောက် အဓိပ္ပါယ်ဖြစ်သည်


Important note on tenses in after clause

after ပါသောဝါကျတွင် verb ကို perfect tenses (has,have/had + V3) ပုံစံသာမက simple present (V1) နှင့် simple past (V2) တို့ဖြင့်လည်း အသုံးပြုနိုင်သည်ကို သိထားသင့်သည်

အောက်ပါ ဥပမာဝါကျများကိုလေ့လာကြည့်ပါ

◾After I left school, I went to America.
◾I will telephone you after I arrive.



Examples:
1. Aung Aung did his homework. Then, he went to bed.
After doing his homework, Aung Aung went to bed.
After Aung Aung had done his homework, he went to bed.
2. Mother came back from the market and then started to cook.
After coming back from the market, mother started to cook.
After mother had come back from the market, she started to cook.
3. He was beaten by his father, then he never smoked again.
After being beaten by his father, he never smoked again.
After he had been beaten by his father, he never smoked again.
4. The books were covered first. Then, they were sent to the library.
After being covered, the books were sent to the library.
After the books had been covered, they were sent to the library.
5. Nyi Lay went out to play with his friends when he had studied his lessons.
After Nyi Lay had studied his lessons, he went out to play with his friends.
Exercises
1. She made the tea and then she laid the table.

▶ After .....

After making the tea, she laid the table.
(OR)
After she had made the tea, she laid the table.

2. Tun Tun sold off all his eggs. Then he went back to his house.

▶ After Tun Tun .....

After Tun Tun had sold off all his eggs, he went back to his house.

3. She ate crabs for dinner and then she began to feel ill.

▶ After .....

After eating crabs for dinner, she began to feel ill.
(OR)
After she had eaten crabs for dinner, she began to feel ill.

4. She read the e-mail from her friend and then sent a reply.

▶ After she .....

After she had read the e-mail from her friend, she sent a reply.

5. He revised all the lessons and then went to school.

▶ After .....

After revising all the lessons, he went to school.
(OR)
After he had revised all the lessons, he went to school.

6. Kyaw Kyaw studied his lessons and then he went out.

▶ After Kyaw Kyaw .....

After Kyaw Kyaw had studied his lessons, he went out.

7. She introduced her name by herself first, and then she presented her paper interestingly.

▶ After .....

After introducing her name by herself, she presented her papers interestingly.
(OR)
After she had introduced her name by herself, she presented the papers interestingly.

8. She took Decolgen for her cold and then she felt better.

▶ After she .....

After she had taken Decolgen for her cold, she felt better.

9. My uncle exercises in the morning and then leaves for work.

▶ After .....

After exercising in the morning, my uncle leaves for work.
(OR)
After my uncle had exercised in the morning, he leaves for work.

10. The actress put on make-up and then she went into the studio.

▶ After the actress .....

After the actress had put on make-up, she went into the studio.
